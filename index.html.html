Guild Updates

Guild Raid 2 Beta

  • Guild Raid 2 Beta is launching.
  • The gameplay mechanics of Guild Raid are being updated.

Floating Castle

  • Both existing and new Floating Castles will feature a Lunar New Year theme.
  • Duration: From the end of maintenance on 2025-01-23 to 2025-02-20 09:59:59.

Special Costume

  • A special costume package, "Paimon Pajama Layne," for the Destroyer Paimon will be added.

Events

1) New Year Attendance Event

  • A New Year attendance event will begin.
  • Rewards include the Zodiac Snake New Year Knight Costume, Awakening Reset Stone, and other various prizes.

This event runs separately from the regular attendance check and lasts for 14 days, with an additional 20 days to claim rewards after the event ends.

2) Ancient Giant Gacha Machine Event

  • An ancient gacha machine has been discovered in Kamazon Land.
  • This event allows players to spend Gacha Coins to earn various rewards.
  • During the event, complete missions to earn coins and spin the capsule gacha machine.
  • Collect empty capsules to exchange for a variety of prizes.

3) Blacksmith Discount Event

  • During the event, the gold cost for services provided by the blacksmith will be discounted.

4) Guild Raid: "Circus of Terror"

  • A new season of Guild Raid is being prepared.

The Guild Raid 2 Beta system will be applied starting with the new season. The Guild Raid season may be adjusted based on future testing or planning updates.

5) Guardian Pass

  • A new season of the Guardian Pass will be available.
  • For the next season, the Platinum Pass rewards will include the option to select one of the following exclusive weapons:
    • Repentance
    • Sun Mountain Boxing Manual
    • Spirit Jade
    • Lotus Gauntlets
    • Dragon Sky Blade
    • Dragon Fang
